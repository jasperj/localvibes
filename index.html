Why Local Vibes?

In May 2017, on a family trip to NYC, we toured the 9/11 memorial in the afternoon and saw Come From Away on Broadway in the evening. The combination of the memorial and a show about the humanity in the aftermath of 9/11 was not intentional, but they complemented each other perfectly to create a truly profound experience. While seeing a show, eating at a restaurant, or going to a bar can be a good experience, I believe the best experiences are a combination of complementary activities.


What is it?

For people who are planning a date or entertaining a friend visiting, Local Vibes makes it easy to quickly discover great local experiences. From classic pairings like tacos and a tequila bar, to a walk through central park for a sandwhich at Tavern on the Green, the complementary activities elevate the whole experience.

VIBE: Lazy Sunday ‍😎

Some people run marathons on Sundays. Not this Vibe. Relax with delicious food, a massage, and minimal walking.

🍳Order a beef sausage breakfast sandwhich to go from C&B Cafe.

👀Find a bench and people watch in Tompkins Square Park.

🦶Get a foot massage at Shangri-La spa

🍩Walk to Ray's Candy Shop for dessert - Oreo beignets and deep fried oreos.
