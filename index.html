Why VIBE?

On a family trip to NYC in 2017, we toured the 9/11 Memorial Museum and in the evening saw Come From Away on Broadway (an inspriring show about humanity post-9/11). The combination complemented each other perfectly to create a truly profound experience. It was a very patriotic vibe and stands out as one of my best life experiences. Wouldn't it be great to easily find the perfect combinations of activities in your city?


What is a VIBE?

A VIBE is a specific combo of a few related things to do and see, curated by people like you. With little effort you can discover and choose themed local experiences you wouldn't find elswehere. It's perfect for planning a casual first date, entertaining a friend, exploring a neighborhood, or even surviving quarantine.
